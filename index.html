Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Washoni before we launch.

When exactly will Washoni launch?

Washoni is scheduled to launch in May 2025. Join our waitlist to be the first to know the exact launch date and get early access.

Will Washoni be available in my city?

We're launching initially in major metropolitan areas and will be expanding quickly. Sign up for early access to see if your city is included in our first phase.

Is the app free for customers?

Yes, the basic version of Washoni will be free for customers. We'll also offer a premium subscription with additional features for fashion enthusiasts.

How much does it cost for tailors?

For tailors, we'll offer tiered pricing based on business size and needs. Early adopters will receive special discounted rates. Join our waitlist for details.

How does the measurement sharing work?

Customers can securely store their measurements in the app and choose which tailors to share them with. This eliminates the need for repeated measuring sessions.

Can I integrate Washoni with my existing systems?

Yes, Washoni will offer API access for seamless integration with popular accounting, inventory, and POS systems for our Professional and Enterprise tier users.
