Our Story

How Washoni came to be and our mission to transform the tailoring industry in Kenya.

Revolutionizing the Tailoring Industry

Washoni was born out of a simple observation: finding reliable tailors in Kenya is challenging, while talented tailors struggle to efficiently manage their businesses and reach new customers.

Our founder, Zaddoch Ponde, experienced these frustrations firsthand. After struggling to find a reliable tailor for a wedding suit and witnessing his family's tailoring business face operational challenges, Zaddoch recognized an opportunity to bridge this gap.

In 2024, with a team of passionate technologists and tailoring experts, Washoni was created to connect customers with skilled tailors while providing tailors with the business tools they need to thrive in the digital age.

Today, Washoni is on a mission to digitize and elevate the tailoring industry across Kenya and eventually all of Africa, preserving the craftsmanship of traditional tailoring while embracing modern technology.

Our Journey

The key milestones in Washoni's development and growth.

January 2024

The concept for Washoni was born, with initial research into the tailoring market in Kenya.

Secured seed funding to develop the Washoni platform and begin working with early adopter tailors.

Scheduled full public launch of the Washoni platform across Kenya.
