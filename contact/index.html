Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about contacting and working with us.

How quickly will you respond to my inquiry?

We strive to respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. For urgent matters, please call our customer service line.

I'm a tailor interested in joining the platform. Who should I contact?

Please use the contact form and select "Tailor interested in the platform" in the dropdown. Our tailor relations team will reach out to you with more information.

Do you have a physical office I can visit?

Yes, our headquarters is located at The Promenade in Westlands, Nairobi. Please contact us in advance to schedule a meeting.

I'm interested in partnering with Washoni. Who should I talk to?

For partnership inquiries, please email partnerships@washoni.com or use the contact form and select "Potential business partner" in the dropdown.
